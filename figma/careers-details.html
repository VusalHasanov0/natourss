Grow with Portonovi Team

Azmont Investments, proud investor of Portonovi Resort, strives to be a company who professionally, efficiently and in a sustainable way manages investments and creates difference by introducing best business practices in Montenegro and the entire region through its business and daughter companies. Therefore, we never stop looking for best and most talented professionals. We are fully aware that only right people in the right place truly make a difference. We are committed to providing a respectful, agile and performance oriented work environment. In our Company differences are embraced, people are valued and ideas and innovation are more than welcome.

With uniqueness in style and perseverance in success, Portonovi Resort is emerging as a luxurious brand with recognizable lifestyle unlike in the region.

On that path, we are creating new perspectives for engaging valuable knowledge, experience and talents in Boka Kotorska Bay and Montenegro.

From the very beginning, we have set strong foundations of Portonovi Resort by taking into employment more than 100 seniors and juniors who are now evolving with us

If you feel like this is the moment for your potentials and enthusiasm to unleash, share your CV and cover letter with us at career@portonovi.com .

We will make sure to keep your CV in our database and contact you in case that your professional background and expertise is considered a match for a future vacant position.
